# Term rollover

Status: ready-for-agent

## Parent

[Academy Portal PRD](../PRD.md)

## What to build

Make re-enrolling for the next Term quick and prompted.

- When re-enrollment for a new Term opens, email Account Holders a reminder.
- Carry a Student's standing Availability from the prior Term into the new Term as an editable starting point.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Opening a new Term for re-enrollment sends reminder emails to Account Holders with prior-Term Students.
- [ ] A returning Student's standing Availability is pre-populated for the new Term and remains editable.
- [ ] Integration test covers the reminder send and availability carry-over.
