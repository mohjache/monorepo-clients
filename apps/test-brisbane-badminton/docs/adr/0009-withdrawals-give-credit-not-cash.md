# Withdrawals give account credit, not cash refunds

When a Student withdraws mid-Term or drops a Plan, their remaining **unattended** Lessons convert to **Credit** on the Account, redeemable against a future Term's Enrollment. There are no cash refunds by default; an Admin can approve exceptions for genuine hardship (e.g. long-term injury).

This avoids reversing Stripe charges and — critically — BNPL settlements, which are awkward to refund partially. It keeps money within the academy while still giving families fair value for lessons they paid for but could not use. Recorded because "all payments become non-refundable credit" is a deliberate commercial stance a future reader (or a family) might otherwise dispute.
